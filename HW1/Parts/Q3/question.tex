Consider the rotation matrix $R(\theta)\in \SO{2}:$
\begin{gather*}
    R(\theta) = \begin{pmatrix}
        \cos(\theta) & -\sin(\theta) \\ 
        \sin(\theta) & \cos(\theta)
    \end{pmatrix}
\end{gather*}
